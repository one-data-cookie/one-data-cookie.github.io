In spring 2026, I took part in [Makers Club](https://www.muni.cz/uchazeci/vzdelavani-po-cely-zivot/rozvoj-kompetenci/filozoficka-fakulta/28744-makers-club-tvorivost-jako-nastroj-zmeny) at Masaryk University. 
Across workshops at the university, [FabLab Brno](https://www.fablabbrno.cz/),
and [Otevřená dílna Rosice](https://www.facebook.com/p/Otev%C5%99en%C3%A1-d%C3%ADlna-Rosice-61570883936301/),
I reflected upon the role of makerspaces in today's world, as well as tried laser cutting, sewing, heat-transfer printing, woodworking,
and 3D printing. As a final project, I made a birthday gift for my wife: a small 3D-printed printing press based on the [Open Press Project](https://openpressproject.com/blogs/news/how-to-3d-print-your-own-printing-press).
