At [Slido](https://www.slido.com/) offsites, it's a tradition to present subjects that fascinate us.
In 2024, I gave ["Those Who Can, Do; Those Who Can’t, Teach"](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/102q9YcA9-NbcMFPAymZiqbcYfqKHz8n_ivueR3iW-DA/edit), arguing the opposite – that those who can, teach.
In 2026, I followed up with ["What 14-Year-Olds Taught Me About Humans"](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1gn4WknVWyDMQmLMLZs1fgJDc7-t8cqMkUelAUHmEJtU/edit?usp=sharing), reflecting on what I have learnt from teenagers about freedom, warmth, and attention.
