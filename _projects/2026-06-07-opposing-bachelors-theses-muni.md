Since 2021, I have served as an opponent for several bachelor's theses at the [Faculty of Arts at Masaryk University](https://www.phil.muni.cz/en),
mostly focused on datavis, data literacy, and data journalism. Specifically, they have explored topics such as validated testing of
visualisation literacy, digital gardens, dataset localisation for benchmarking LLMs, and accessibility in Czech data journalism.
